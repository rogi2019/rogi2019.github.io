Projects

Reach Out and Give is successfully running or helping out several projects specially in education for kids. We are committed to invest and help run volunteer organizations across the world.

  • USA
  • India

SPVM (Swami Pranavananda Vidya Mandir) Ukhimath, Uttarakhand India

An English-Medium coeducational school in the hills of Uttarakhand, run by Bharat Sevasram Sangha, it caters to the impoverished children of the area. It depends primarily on private contributions. Upon visiting the school, Ratna Karmakar was deeply impressed by the quality of education and the sincerity of the children attending the school. At her recommendation, Reach Out & Give International has been giving it an annual donation of $1800 to $2000 (depending upon the exchange rate) since 2012. The school always needs volunteer teachers. If anyone is interested in serving the children, they can contact Ratna at board@roginternational.org or Sudhir Bramhacahri at 91-94-1012-7123. Please be aware of the challenges to live there amongst its modest facilities and limited availability of food. One has to be really committed to service if he/she wants to participate in the voluntary work.

Ramakrishna Sarada Mission, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh India

In 2019, we gave a one-time grant of $5000 (in Indian currency) for the operational expenses of the girls' school.

Ghatmura Vivekanada Project India

Ghatmura is a remote village in the district of Paschim Midnapur. Ghatmura Vivekananda Patriarchate, a Goverment Registered Voluntary Social Welfare Organization has been rendering humanitarian services at and around Ghatmura since 1982. Reach Out & Give Int'l has been supporting infrastructure projects related to the organization's healthcare facilities. Our members have visited this place and were very impressed with their activities in helping the underprivileged folks in rural West Bengal.

Metiaburuz mother-daughter Pre-school, Kolkata India

The Rotary Club of Salt Lake has sponsored this unique and worthwhile project to educate illiterate mothers alongside their daughters in a slum area in South Kolkata. Reach Out & Give Int'l provides full financial support to run the program. We have adopted this project in 2012.

AIWC North Kolkata All India Women's Conference is a respectable NGO which aids the needy people of Kolkata and surrounding areas by providing healthcare and eduction. Reach Out & Give International has been involved with their activities for the past few years. We have funded the construction of two bathrooms for two pre-schools in North Kolkata. We also help with nutritional meals for several children at one of the pre-schools. Beginning 2020, we no longer support them directly.

Rotary Club of Salt Lake and Bidhan Nagar India

We provide funding for five individual's educational needs , four of them attend school and one attends college. The Rotary club monitors their progress and distributes the money. In collaboration with the Rotary Club we also provide some children with new outfits during the Durga Puja in October of each year.

Belgharia Ramakrishna Mission India

The monk incharge of the center provides villagers with solar lanterns which help tremendously in their studies by allowing them to prepare for school work where they do not have access to electricity. The monk also trains the users to maintain the lanterns and fix them as necessary. We support this noble work with a minimal annual contribution which provides, perhaps, the cost for 12 lanterns a year. This project is currently dormant.

Vivek Society of Purbachal, Salt Lake India

Vivek Society of Purbachal, Salt Lake is an NGO which helps in the education of impoverished children. This year we have joined them and supported two of their projects. a) We give about $300 to Ramakrishna Mission, Belgharia to provide solar lanterns to poor villagers in one district in North 24 Parganas. The Mission not only provides the villagers with the lanterns. It also trains them to maintain and repair them. We believe this is a very essential tool for the children to study in the evening and to help the whole family rune their chores little comfortably. b) We provide approximately $150 for scholarships for youth in need

Lion's Club, Jalpaiguri, West BengalIndia

Lion's Club works with an orphanage for girls in the city of Jalpaiguri. Our board members have visited the orphanage and were very impressed by the quality of care provided to the girls. We have been supporting their activities by providing monetary and in-kind help since 2015. The monetary donation is Rs.25,000/year.
