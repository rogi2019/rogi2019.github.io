REACH OUT AND GIVE INTERNATIONAL, INC (ROG-I) was founded in 2008 by Ratna Karmakar, an educationist and a social worker, in New Jersey.  It is approved as a (501)(c)(3) tax-exempt organization in the US.  Its goals include fostering health and education amongst needy children, providing monetary and in-kind help, and constantly recruiting committed volunteers to teach kids English, math and computers.
